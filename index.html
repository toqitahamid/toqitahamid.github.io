Hello, my name is Toqi Tahamid Sarker. I am a co-founder of Penguin, an e-commerce platform based in Bangladesh focusing on consumer electronics. I am passionate about utilizing deep learning techniques to address real-world challenges. I created a large-scale solar dataset with over 500,000 images to track solar events using deep learning. I ranked top 17% in The Nature Conservancy Fisheries Monitoring competition at Kaggle.

I worked at Panacea Lab under the supervision of Professor Juan M. Banda at Georgia State University, and published a paper in IEEE Big Data 2019.

I worked under the supervision of Mr. Moin Mostakim at BRAC University on a deep learning project as part of my undergraduate thesis requirement.

My research interests are:

  • Applied Deep Learning
  • Big Data Mining
  • Human-Centered AI
  • Recommender System
